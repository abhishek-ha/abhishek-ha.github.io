Little Things That Can Improve Your Health

Incorporating small but important things into your daily routine can improve your overall health. Some examples of these habits include getting up and moving every half hour to reduce the risks of chronic disease associated with prolonged sitting, staying hydrated, having a nutritious snack every few hours, practising mindfulness...

The Dangers of a Sedentary Lifestyle

It is well-established that a sedentary lifestyle, characterized by long periods of sitting, is linked to various health problems such as heart disease, stroke, diabetes, cancer, brain shrinkage, muscle loss, poor posture, back pain, and early death. These risks apply to anyone who spends a lot of time sitting, regardless of the reason, including work, travel, fatigue, illness, or leisure.

What Is A Full Body Health Checkup?

A full body checkup, also known as a comprehensive health assessment or complete physical examination, is a thorough medical evaluation that assesses the overall health and well-being of an individual. It usually involves a series of tests and procedures to check various body systems and functions, including the heart, lungs, abdomen, ears, nose, and throat, as well as blood, urine, and other body fluids.
