The Dangers of a Sedentary Lifestyle

It is well-established that a sedentary lifestyle, characterized by long periods of sitting, is linked to various health problems such as heart disease, stroke, diabetes, cancer, brain shrinkage, muscle loss, poor posture, back pain, and early death. These risks apply to anyone who spends a lot of time sitting, regardless of the reason, including work, travel, fatigue, illness, or leisure.

A full body checkup, also known as a comprehensive health assessment or complete physical examination, is a thorough medical evaluation that assesses the overall health and well-being of an individual.
